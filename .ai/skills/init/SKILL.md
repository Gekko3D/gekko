# Skill: Init AI Scaffold

Use this skill when the repo’s AI scaffold looks incomplete or damaged.

## Steps

1. Verify that `.ai/`, `.ai/docs/`, `.ai/features/`, `.ai/adr/`, and `.ai/skills/` exist.
2. Verify that `.ai/context.md`, `.ai/conventions.md`, `.ai/RULES.md`, and `.ai/known-issues.md` exist.
3. If files are missing, recreate only the missing scaffold pieces.
4. Do not overwrite repo-specific content unless the engineer explicitly asks for a refresh.
5. Keep `AGENTS.md` as the primary Codex contract for this repo.
